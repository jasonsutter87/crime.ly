About us

Why Crime.ly

Violent crime is prevalent throughout much of the bay area, but it is hard to know where this occurs. SF Open Data provides information on all the crimes commited in San Francisco, but this data is hard to work through. We wanted to provide an easy to view map that allows users to view where crimes are more common in the city. This will allow our friends and users to stay safe out on the streets and hopefully prevent more violent crime in our community.
